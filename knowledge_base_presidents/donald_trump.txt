Donald Trump – 47th President of the United States

Donald John Trump was born on June 14, 1946, in Queens, New York City.
He is serving his second term as President of the United States, having been inaugurated on January 20, 2025.
He previously served as the 45th President from 2017 to 2021.

Key facts:
- Trump is the first US president to serve two non-consecutive terms since Grover Cleveland.
- Before entering politics he was a real estate developer and television personality, best known for hosting The Apprentice.
- He founded The Trump Organization, a privately held conglomerate involved in real estate, hotels, and golf resorts.
- In 2016 he defeated Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton in the presidential election.
- In 2024 he defeated Democratic nominee Kamala Harris to win his second term.
- His political platform is associated with the "America First" policy agenda.
- He was the first US president to be impeached twice by the House of Representatives (2019 and 2021), though he was acquitted by the Senate both times.
- Trump was born and raised in New York but is registered to vote in Florida, where Mar-a-Lago, his private club and residence, is located.
